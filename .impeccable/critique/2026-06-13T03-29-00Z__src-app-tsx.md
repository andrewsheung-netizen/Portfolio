## Priority Issues

- [P2] Flat-day state is noisy. When nothing moved since last capture, movers line shows "VOO HK$0 · NVDA HK$0 · AAPL HK$0" and Day cells read "HK$0" — violates the system's own silence-when-nothing principle. Fix: suppress zero/near-zero movers; if all flat, hide line or collapse to "Flat today."
- [P2] Esc / backdrop click silently discards in-progress form edits. The one gap in the destructive-guard story. Fix: when form is dirty, intercept close with a "Discard changes?" confirm.
- [P3] Delete still has no undo. Bulk ops guarded, sells/closes reversible, but position delete is permanent. Fix (optional): toast-undo or fold into trade/undo ledger.
- [P3] Power-user gaps: no column sorting, no ticker autofill on Add, no bulk actions.
- [P3] "as of HH:MM" overstates freshness after a keyless refresh that fetched nothing. Fix: stamp only on successful fetch; else show last real fetch or "prices manual."
